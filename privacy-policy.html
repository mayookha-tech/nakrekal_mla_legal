Privacy Policy for VV Connect (Nakrekal Voice)

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VV Connect (Nakrekal Voice). This mobile application is designed to facilitate communication between the citizens of the Nakrekal constituency and the office of MLA Vemula Veeresham. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number exclusively for secure login verification via OTP (One-Time Password) and account management.
  • Photos/Images (Optional): If you choose to submit a civic complaint, you may optionally upload photos using your device camera or gallery to provide visual proof of the issue.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To process and forward your civic complaints and callback requests to the designated camp office staff.
  • To notify you about important constituency events or updates.

4. Data Security & Storage

Your data is securely transmitted using encrypted protocols (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase). We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide civic services.

  • Account Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. You can request deletion directly within the application or by contacting our support team.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact the MLA Camp Office at Nakrekal or reach out via the in-app callback request feature.

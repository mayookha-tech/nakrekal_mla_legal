Privacy Policy for Vemula Veeresham MLA

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vemula Veeresham MLA (Application Package ID: com.mayookha.mla.vveeresham). This mobile application is designed to facilitate communication between citizens and the office of MLA Vemula Veeresham. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our civic communication services, we collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number exclusively for secure login verification via OTP (One-Time Password) and account management.
  • Photos/Images (Optional): If you choose to submit a civic complaint or feedback, you may optionally upload photos using your device camera or gallery to provide visual proof of the issue.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To process and forward your civic complaints, requests, and callback inquiries to the designated camp office staff.
  • To notify you about important events, developmental updates, or information relevant to your area.

4. Data Security & Storage

Your data is securely transmitted using encrypted protocols (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase). We enforce strict access controls and do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers or commercial entities.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill civic administrative processes and provide ongoing services.

  • Account Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. You can initiate this request directly within the application settings or by contacting our support team via email.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, wish to request manual data deletion, or require technical support, please contact the MLA Camp Office or reach out to our technical publisher directly at support@mayookha.co.in.
